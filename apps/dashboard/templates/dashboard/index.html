Systemic Risk focuses on how illiquidity, insolvency, and losses propogate through connections in the financial system.

We focus on four different types of institutions: brokers/dealers, hedge funds, insurance companies, and banks.
We look at the 25 largest firms in each sector.

Using publically available data, we find the monthly returns of these firms and
apply principal components analysis and granger-causality tests.
