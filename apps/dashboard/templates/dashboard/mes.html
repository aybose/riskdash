Marginal and Systemic Expected Shortfall

This work is based on Acharya, Engle, and Richardson, 2012, "Capital Shortfall: A New Approach to Ranking and Regulating Systemic Risks".

The financial crisis of 2007-2009 has given way to the sovereign debt crisis of 2010-2012, yet many of the banking issues remain the same. This method estimates the capital that a financial firm would need to raise in case of another financial crisis. This measure summarizes the major characteristics of systemic risk and provides a reliable interpretation of the past and current financial crisis.
